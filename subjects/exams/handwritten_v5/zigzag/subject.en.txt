Write a program that takes one string argument and prints it in zigzag case.

Characters at even indexes must be uppercase. Characters at odd indexes must be lowercase. Index starts at 0. Non-alphabetic characters are printed unchanged but still count in the index.

Examples:

```txt
$> ./zigzag hello | cat -e
HeLlO$
$> ./zigzag "a b c" | cat -e
A B C$
```

Allowed functions: `write`.
